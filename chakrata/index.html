About Chakrata

Chakrata is an off-beat cantonment town tucked away in the hills of Dehradun district. Far from the tourist crowds of Mussoorie and Shimla, Chakrata offers a raw, untouched wilderness experience that few places in Uttarakhand can match.

The star attraction is Tiger Falls — at 312 feet, it is one of the highest waterfalls in India. Deoban offers dense deodar forests and snow in winter. The ancient Budher Caves are mysterious limestone formations waiting to be explored. Chilmiri Neck is the perfect sunset point, and Kanasar offers pristine camping amidst deodar forests. Chakrata is very less crowded, making it the perfect digital detox destination for those seeking peace and nature.

Chakrata Trip Itinerary (2 Days)

Day 1

Drive from Dehradun, Tiger Falls Trek & Chilmiri Neck Sunset

Morning drive from Dehradun to Chakrata (90 km). After check-in, head to Tiger Falls for a scenic trek through the forest to the waterfall base. Evening visit to Chilmiri Neck for a stunning sunset over the Himalayas.

Distance: ~90 km from Dehradun | Drive: 3-4 hrs
Day 2

Deoban, Budher Caves, Kanasar & Return

Early morning drive to Deoban for breathtaking views from the dense deodar forest. Explore the ancient Budher Caves. Visit Kanasar for a peaceful forest walk. Drive back to Dehradun by evening.

Deoban: ~15 km from Chakrata

Best Time to Visit Chakrata

Chakrata can be visited throughout the year, each season offering a different experience:

  • March - June: Pleasant weather, lush greenery, ideal for trekking and waterfalls. Temperature: 10°C to 25°C.
  • September - November: Post-monsoon clarity, great views, comfortable weather.
  • December - February: Snowfall at Deoban and higher areas. Temperature: -2°C to 10°C.

Avoid July-August due to heavy rains and slippery trails near waterfalls.

Things to Carry

  • Comfortable trekking shoes with good grip
  • Warm layers — Chakrata gets cold even in summer evenings
  • Rain poncho / umbrella
  • Torch/flashlight for cave exploration
  • Water bottle, dry snacks, energy bars
  • Valid ID proof
  • Cash — ATM availability is very limited

Important Tips

  • Chakrata is a cantonment area — some zones may require permits
  • Mobile network is patchy — BSNL works best
  • Tiger Falls trek is moderate (1.5 km) — wear proper shoes
  • Budher Caves require a torch — carry your own
  • Book accommodation in advance during long weekends
  • Roads to Deoban can be rough — our Winger handles them well
