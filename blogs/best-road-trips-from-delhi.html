10 Best Road Trips from Delhi

By Tata Winger — The Ultimate 2026 Guide for Groups & Families

Updated April 2026 8 min read From Delhi

Delhi is the perfect starting point for some of India's most epic road trips. Whether you're looking for mountains, temples, adventure, or just a weekend getaway — there's a road trip for every mood. And when you're traveling with a group of 8-14 people, there's nothing better than a Tata Winger — comfortable, spacious, and affordable.

Here are the 10 best road trips from Delhi that you can do with Nainital AH Tour:

1. Delhi to Mussoorie — Queen of Hills

Distance: ~290 km | Drive: 6-7 hours | Best for: Families, couples

Mussoorie is the classic hill station getaway from Delhi. Mall Road, Kempty Falls, Gun Hill, and Cloud's End — all within easy reach. The drive through Dehradun is scenic and comfortable in our Tata Winger.

Read our complete Mussoorie guide →

2. Delhi to Rishikesh & Haridwar — Adventure + Spiritual

Distance: ~240 km | Drive: 5-6 hours | Best for: Friends, adventure groups

The perfect weekend combo — morning Ganga Aarti at Haridwar, afternoon river rafting at Rishikesh, evening bungee jumping. Our Winger drops you at every point without the hassle of multiple cabs.

Read our complete Rishikesh guide →

3. Delhi to Nainital — City of Lakes

Distance: ~300 km | Drive: 6-7 hours | Best for: Families, honeymoon

Naini Lake boating, Snow View Point, Tiffin Top, and the famous Mall Road. Combine with Bhimtal, Sattal, and Naukuchiatal for a 4-5 day trip. The Tata Winger handles the Kumaon hills with ease.

Read our complete Nainital guide →

4. Delhi to Kedarnath — Sacred Pilgrimage

Distance: ~460 km (to Gaurikund) | Drive: 12-14 hours | Best for: Pilgrim groups

One of the most spiritually powerful road trips in India. The drive through Devprayag, Rudraprayag, and the Mandakini Valley is breathtaking. Our experienced hill drivers make this long journey safe and comfortable.

Read our complete Kedarnath guide →

5. Delhi to Kashmir — Paradise on Earth

Distance: ~850 km | Drive: 2 days | Best for: Groups, extended trips

The ultimate road trip — Delhi to Jammu to Srinagar via the Jawahar Tunnel. Dal Lake, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg — 6-8 days of pure paradise. The Tata Winger is perfect for this long journey with a group.

Read our complete Kashmir guide →

6. Delhi to Khatu Shyam Ji — Devotion in Rajasthan

Distance: ~320 km | Drive: 6-7 hours | Best for: Pilgrim groups, bhajan mandali

A very popular pilgrimage from Delhi. Shyam Baba's temple in Sikar, Rajasthan attracts millions of devotees. Combine with Salasar Balaji for a complete darshan trip.

Read our complete Khatu Shyam guide →

7. Delhi to Ajmer & Pushkar — Sufi & Sacred

Distance: ~400 km | Drive: 6-7 hours | Best for: All faiths, families

Visit the revered Ajmer Sharif Dargah and the only Brahma Temple in the world at Pushkar — just 14 km apart. Add Jaipur for a complete Rajasthan circuit.

Read our complete Ajmer guide →

8. Delhi to Ayodhya — Ram Janmabhoomi

Distance: ~630 km | Drive: 10-11 hours | Best for: Pilgrim groups, families

Since the grand Ram Mandir inauguration, Ayodhya has become one of the most visited destinations from Delhi. Hanuman Garhi, Saryu Aarti, and the magnificent temple complex — a divine experience.

Read our complete Ayodhya guide →

9. Delhi to Jim Corbett — Wildlife Safari

Distance: ~260 km | Drive: 5-6 hours | Best for: Families, nature lovers

India's oldest national park and the best place to spot Bengal Tigers. Our Winger drops you at the park gate. Book jeep safari separately. Best visited Nov-June.

Read our complete Jim Corbett guide →

10. Delhi to Char Dham — The Ultimate Yatra

Distance: ~1,500+ km total circuit | Drive: 10-12 days | Best for: Devotees, families

Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, Badrinath — the complete Char Dham circuit is the most sacred road trip in India. Our Tata Winger and experienced hill driver will be your companion throughout this life-changing journey.

Read our complete Char Dham guide →
