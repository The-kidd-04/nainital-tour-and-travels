About Kashmir

Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is one of the most beautiful destinations in the world. Nestled in the Himalayan mountains, this enchanting valley is known for its pristine Dal Lake, snow-capped peaks, lush gardens, and warm hospitality.

From the Mughal Gardens of Srinagar to the skiing slopes of Gulmarg, the pine forests of Pahalgam to the glaciers of Sonmarg — Kashmir offers an experience that stays with you forever. The valley is also famous for its saffron fields, Kashmiri cuisine, and exquisite handicrafts.

Kashmir Trip Itinerary (6-8 Days)

Day 1

Delhi to Jammu

Early morning departure from Delhi in our Tata Winger. Drive via NH44 to Jammu. Overnight stay at Jammu. Optional visit to Vaishno Devi base (Katra).

Distance: ~580 km | Drive: 9-10 hrs
Day 2

Jammu to Srinagar

Drive through the stunning Jawahar Tunnel and Banihal Pass. Arrive in Srinagar by evening. Check into houseboat on Dal Lake. Evening Shikara ride.

Distance: ~270 km | Drive: 7-8 hrs
Day 3

Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Visit Mughal Gardens — Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, Chashme Shahi. Afternoon visit to Shankaracharya Temple. Evening Dal Lake Shikara ride and local market.

Local sightseeing | Full day
Day 4

Srinagar to Gulmarg

Drive to Gulmarg — "Meadow of Flowers." Gondola ride to Khilanmarg and Apharwat Peak (4,200m). Enjoy skiing (winter) or meadow walks (summer). Return to Srinagar.

Distance: ~50 km | Drive: 1.5 hrs
Day 5

Srinagar to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam — "Valley of Shepherds." Visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari. Enjoy pony rides and riverside picnics. Overnight at Pahalgam.

Distance: ~90 km | Drive: 2.5 hrs
Day 6

Pahalgam to Srinagar / Sonmarg (Optional)

Return to Srinagar or drive to Sonmarg — "Meadow of Gold." Visit Thajiwas Glacier. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Distance: ~80-170 km | Drive: 2-5 hrs
Day 7-8

Return Journey — Srinagar to Delhi

Early morning departure. Drive back via Jammu with overnight halt. Arrive Delhi by next evening. Or take a flight from Srinagar (we drop you at airport).

Distance: ~850 km | Drive: 2 days

Best Time to Visit Kashmir

  • March - May: Spring season with tulip blooms, pleasant weather (10°C - 25°C). Tulip Garden is a must-visit.
  • June - August: Summer, perfect for sightseeing. Green valleys, comfortable weather (15°C - 30°C).
  • September - November: Autumn with golden Chinar trees. Fewer crowds, beautiful colors.
  • December - February: Winter wonderland. Snow in Gulmarg, skiing season. Cold (-5°C to 5°C).

Things to Carry

  • Warm clothes — even in summer, evenings can be cold
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses (high UV at altitude)
  • Rain jacket / umbrella
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID — mandatory at checkpoints)
  • Cash — some areas have limited ATM/UPI access
  • Camera — you'll need it everywhere!

Important Tips

  • Always negotiate Shikara and pony ride prices before starting
  • Book Gulmarg Gondola tickets online to avoid long queues
  • Carry your ID — there are multiple army checkpoints on the highway
  • Try Kashmiri Wazwan cuisine — Rogan Josh, Dum Aloo, Kahwa tea
  • Buy saffron only from authorized shops (avoid tourist traps)
  • Jio and Airtel work in most of Srinagar; BSNL is best in remote areas
